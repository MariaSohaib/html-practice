Low and Slow

posted by BGB,November 15,2016

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vaccum-scaled in plastic) into a water hath that is precisely set to target the temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In this book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potther describe it as "ultra-low-temperature-poaching."

Next month, we will be serving Sous Vide Salmon with Dill Hollandaise. To reserve a seat at chef table, contact us before November 30.
